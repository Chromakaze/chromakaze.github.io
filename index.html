I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada. I lived a simple life, nothing much else to elaborate on from that front.

Since I was a child, I've always had such an interest in technology; though video games caught my undivided attention. I was used to the idea of things moving on a screen due to television, but when I picked up that NES remote and was given the ability to influence what would happen on screen felt unreal. As I got exposed to more and more games from different platforms, my passion for video games began to grow more.

As I got older, I began to ponder about the creation of video games. I remember looking through games that allowed me to view concept art and scrapped concepts as unlockable items I can view in a menu. The things that could've been; the vision of something else, potentially something grander. As time marched forward, and using my skills in mathematics and logistics, I wanted to be a programmer. Being able to apply my work for creative means and being able to share that passion for this medium; these stories where I've collaborated with others to weave this elaborate piece of art. I genuinely believe that they are the most powerful artform to tell stories, and is the leading artform of the 21st century.
